Pasta Carbonara

Description

The classic Italian pasta carbonara recipe, tested and perfected! This comforting dish features spaghetti in a creamy sauce with eggs

Delicious! Yum! Yum!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Use eggs at room temperature. Make sure you remove the eggs from the fridge half an hour before you get to cooking.
  2. Whisk the eggs to perfection.
  3. Boiling the pasta.
  4. Cook the bacon.
  5. Add the cheese to the eggs.
  6. Be careful when adding the sauce.
