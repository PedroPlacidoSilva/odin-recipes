Lasagna

Description

A rich and creamy whole-wheat pasta dish filled layer by layer with refreshingly fresh onions and garlic, lathered in a succulent sauce and topped with imported, premium quality mozzarella.

Very tasty! Yum! Yum!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Start by setting a large pot of water to boil
  2. Once the water starts to boil, add the noodles to the pot and stir them continuously for 2 minutes so they don't stick to the sides
  3. Then, leave the noodles to cook for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring them occasionally
